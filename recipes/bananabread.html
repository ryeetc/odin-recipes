Banana Bread

The Best Banana Bread

This is a sure-fire recipe for a wonderful banana bread. It's simple and foolproof!

This recipe can also be easily made vegan by swapping out the egg for one ripe banana.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl; mix to combine
  3. Beat egg well
  4. Mash bananas to desired consistency
  5. Add bananas, egg, and vegetable oil to dry ingredients. Use a cake spatula to mix
  6. Throughly grease a loaf pan and add mix
  7. PLace the loaf pan in the oven for 1 hour
  8. Remove from oven and let cool on rack for at least half an hour
