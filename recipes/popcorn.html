Popcorn

The Best Popcorn

This is an incredibly difficult recipe to make mediocre popcorn.

You ready?

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of microwave popcorn
  • Toppings of choice if you're feeling crazy

Steps:

  1. Place the bag of popcorn in the microwave for the amount of time indicated on the package
  2. Add desired toppings. I reccomend some nutritional yeast.
  3. Serve and enjoy. You did it! Well done!
