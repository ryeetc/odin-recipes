Sweet and Sour Tofu

The Best Sweet and Sour Tofu

This is a simple, quick, and healthy recipe for a vegan meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of extra frim tofu
  • 1 tbsp vegan butter or vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables
  • 2 cups rice (rinsed)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 cups soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Start rice in a rice cooker
  2. Preheat a non-stick pan on medium-high heat
  3. Chop tofu into 0.5 inch cubes
  4. Finely chop garlic and add to pan with butter or oil
  5. Once garlic is roasted, add maple syrup, gochujang, and soy sauce. Stire to combine
  6. Add tofu to pan and gently mix until they are well-coated
  7. Add vegetables to pan and gently stir
  8. Cook until mixture in pan is reduced and sticky (about ten minutes)
  9. Add rice vinegar, salt, and sugar to rice once finished
  10. Add to a bowl and enjoy!
